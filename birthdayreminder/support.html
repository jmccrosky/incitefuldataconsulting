Support

Need help with BirthdayReminder? You're in the right place.

General Questions

Is the app really free?

Yes! The core birthday tracking features are completely free with no limitations. You only need Premium ($0.99/month or $4.99/year) if you want AI-powered import features or cloud sync.

Do I need to create an account?

No! The app works completely without an account. You only need to sign in with Apple if you want cloud sync (Premium feature).

What data do you collect?

For free users: minimal anonymous usage analytics. Your birthdays stay only on your device. For Premium users who opt into cloud sync: birthdays are stored securely on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use the app offline?

Absolutely. The free version works 100% offline. Premium users can also use the app offline after syncing.

What platforms are supported?

Currently iOS only (iPhone). iPad and Android may come in the future.

Features & Usage

Birthday Management

How do I add a birthday?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter the person's name and birthday, and optionally add a nickname or notes.

Can I add birthdays without a birth year?

Yes! Birth year is optional. You can just enter the month and day if you don't know someone's birth year.

How do notifications work?

The app sends you local notifications before upcoming birthdays. You can customize notification timing in Settings.

Premium Features

What is AI-powered import?

Snap a photo of birthday invitations, handwritten lists, or upload CSV files and spreadsheets—AI automatically extracts the birthdays in any date format. Perfect for digitizing paper lists, party invitations, or old spreadsheets. You review and confirm before adding them to your app.

How many AI imports do I get?

Premium users get 10 imports per week. Each import can contain multiple birthdays.

What file formats does AI import support?

Photos (JPG, PNG) including handwritten lists and invitations, PDFs, spreadsheets (CSV, Excel), and text files. Maximum file size is 10MB.

How does cloud sync work?

Sign in with Apple (Premium required), and your birthdays will be stored securely in the cloud. Changes sync automatically when you're online.

What happens if my Premium subscription expires?

You keep all your birthdays! If you had cloud sync enabled, we'll automatically download your data to your device before deleting it from our servers. You can continue using the app with local storage.

Data & Privacy

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data. You'll get a CSV file with all your birthdays that you can save or email to yourself. Available to all users.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This will immediately delete all your data from our servers (if using cloud sync) and from your device.

Where is my data stored?

Free users: only on your device. Premium users with cloud sync: encrypted on Cloudflare's global network.

Does AI import use my data for training?

We use Google Gemini AI for birthday extraction. According to Google's policy, data sent to their API may be used for service improvement but not for ad targeting. Your imported data is deleted from our servers immediately after processing.

Subscription & Billing

How much does Premium cost?

Monthly: $0.99/month
Annual: $4.99/year (save 17%)

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Birthday Reminder → Cancel Subscription. You can also manage subscriptions from within the app.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Contact Apple Support or request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?

Yes! Go to Settings → Manage Subscription in the app, or change it in iOS Settings → Subscriptions.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is automatically saved to your device before being deleted from our servers. You keep everything and can continue using the free version.

Troubleshooting

Notifications aren't working

  1. Make sure you granted notification permissions when first opening the app
  2. Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Birthday Reminder - ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  3. Verify you have at least one upcoming birthday in the app

AI import isn't working

  1. Make sure you have an active Premium subscription
  2. Check that your file is under 10MB
  3. Try using a clearer photo or higher quality scan
  4. Contact support if the issue persists

Cloud sync isn't syncing

  1. Make sure you're signed in with Apple (Settings → Sign In)
  2. Check that you have an internet connection
  3. Try signing out and signing back in
  4. Contact support if sync issues continue

App is crashing

  1. Try force-quitting and reopening the app
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. Contact support with details about when the crash occurs
