Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

The Short Version

  • Free users: Your data stays on your device only. We collect minimal anonymous usage analytics.
  • Premium users: Your data is stored securely in the cloud only if you choose to enable cloud sync.
  • AI import: Files are processed by Google Gemini AI and immediately deleted.
  • Usage analytics: We collect anonymous daily usage stats (device ID, app version, premium status, birthday count).
  • No ads. No tracking. No selling your data.

Overview

BirthdayReminder: Never Forget ("BirthdayReminder" or "the App") is designed with privacy as a priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Who We Are

BirthdayReminder is developed and operated by Inciteful Data Consulting Oy. This is a personal project built to explore AI-assisted development.

Contact: mccrosky@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Free Users (No Account)

What we collect: Anonymous usage analytics (see "Usage Analytics" section below)

Your birthday data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage. We have no access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

We collect minimal anonymous usage statistics to understand app adoption and engagement. This data cannot identify you personally.

Premium Users (With Cloud Sync)

When you sign in with Apple (optional, Premium feature only):

What we collect:

  • Apple User ID (anonymized identifier from Apple)
  • Your name (if you choose to share it during Apple Sign-in)
  • Birthday data you choose to sync (names, dates, notes)

What we DON'T collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your Apple ID password
  • Any other personal information

Where it's stored:

  • Cloudflare Workers (serverless platform)
  • Cloudflare D1 database (encrypted at rest)
  • Servers located globally on Cloudflare's network

AI Import Feature (Premium Only)

How it works:

  1. You upload a photo, PDF, or document
  2. File is sent to our server (Cloudflare Workers)
  3. File is forwarded to Google Gemini AI API for processing
  4. Gemini extracts birthday information
  5. Results are returned to you for review
  6. The original file is immediately deleted from our servers
  7. Only birthdays you confirm are saved

Third-party processing: Files are processed by Google Gemini AI. According to Google's AI API Terms, data sent to their API may be retained temporarily for service improvement and abuse prevention. Google states they do not use API data for ad targeting. We recommend not uploading files containing sensitive information beyond birthday data.

Usage Analytics & Telemetry

We collect minimal anonymous usage analytics to understand how the app is being used and to improve it. This applies to all users (free and Premium).

What We Collect

Once per day, when you open the app, we collect:

  • Device ID (IDFV) - iOS Identifier for Vendor (not personally identifiable)
  • App version (e.g., "1.0.0")
  • Premium status (whether you have an active subscription)
  • Birthday count (how many birthdays you have stored)
  • Date (the day the usage data was sent)

What We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, Apple ID)
  • No birthday data itself (names, dates, notes)
  • No location data
  • No contact information
  • No device model or OS version

Why We Collect This

We use this data to:

  • Count daily active users (DAU) and monthly active users (MAU)
  • Understand which app versions are being used
  • Track Premium conversion and retention
  • Identify engagement trends
  • Make data-informed decisions about features

Data Retention

Free Users

Your data stays on your device indefinitely until you delete the app, manually delete birthdays, or delete all app data via iOS Settings.

Premium Users (Cloud Sync)

Active subscription:

  • Data stored indefinitely while subscription is active
  • You can delete data anytime via Settings → Delete Account

Expired subscription:

  • We automatically download all your cloud data to your device
  • Delete all your data from our servers within 24 hours
  • Revoke cloud sync access
  • You keep all your data locally on your device

Account deletion:

  • Immediate deletion from our servers (cannot be undone)
  • Data remains on your device unless you also delete the app

Your Rights & Controls

Data Export

  • How: Settings → Export Data
  • Format: CSV file with all your birthdays
  • Available to: All users (free and Premium)

Data Deletion

Free users:

  • Delete individual birthdays within the app
  • Delete app to remove all data from device

Premium users:

  • Settings → Delete Account removes all data from servers
  • Delete app to remove all data from device
  • OR: Let subscription expire and data is auto-deleted from servers

Access & Correction

  • You can view all your data within the app anytime
  • Edit birthdays directly in the app
  • Export data to see everything we store

Data Security

On your device:

  • Protected by iOS security features
  • Encrypted by iOS when device is locked

In the cloud (Premium users):

  • Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encrypted at rest (Cloudflare D1 encryption)
  • Secure authentication (Apple Sign-In with JWT tokens)
  • Regular security updates

AI import:

  • Files transmitted over HTTPS
  • Processed in isolated, secure environment
  • Immediately deleted after processing

International Users & Legal Rights

GDPR (European Users)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object

To exercise these rights, use the in-app data export/deletion features or contact us.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete personal information
  • Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising these rights
