About

I'm a Principal Data Scientist and Head of Sustainability and Social Change at Mozilla and Thoughtworks, based in Helsinki, Finland. With over 10 years of experience working with data and statistics, I've had the privilege of contributing to impactful research at organizations including Mozilla, Google, and Statistics Canada.

My work sits at the intersection of technology, policy, and social impact. I lead crowdsourced research initiatives that power Mozilla's policy and advocacy efforts, advise on critical tech policy matters including the Digital Services Act (DSA), and investigate the societal implications of emerging technologies.

Current Focus: Studying the impact of generative AI on elections, evaluating tools for identifying AI-generated content, and advancing sustainable technology practices.
